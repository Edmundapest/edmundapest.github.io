Biei — Patchwork Hills & Blue Pond
Saturday · July 18, 2026
🌸 Flowers 🚗 ~2.5 hrs
  • Morning 🚗
    Depart Sapporo → Biei Drive north on Route 12 alongside the Ishikari River valley

Monday · July 20, 2026
💜 Peak Lavender 🌸 Flowers

    Farm Tomita at dawn — PRIORITY STOP Arrive before 8 AM to catch morning mist floating over the purple rows, before the crowds arrive

Wednesday · July 22, 2026

Before You Go

Trip Notes

🚗 Driving

  • International Driving Permit required
  • Book rental car well in advance
  • Toll roads between major cities
  • ETC card saves time at tolls
  • Google Maps works perfectly

🌸 Flowers

  • Lavender peaks July 15–25 in Furano
  • Farm Tomita: arrive before 8 AM
  • Shikisai-no-oka: tractor rides available
  • Sunflowers face south by afternoon
  • Bring a macro lens for close-ups

🎆 Fireworks

  • Confirm exact dates closer to travel
  • Arrive 1–2 hrs early for a good spot
  • Bring a ground sheet to sit on
  • Hanabi shows last 45–90 minutes
  • Check local boards for exact times

🏨 Staying

  • Book accommodation 3+ months ahead
  • Furano ryokan during lavender season fills fast
  • IC Suica card for city transit
  • Convenience stores are your best friends
  • Bear spray advisable on nature walks
