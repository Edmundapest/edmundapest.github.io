Before You Go

Trip Notes

🚗 Driving

  • International Driving Permit required
  • Book rental car 3+ months ahead
  • ETC card for toll booths
  • Google Maps works great
  • Roads wide and well maintained

💜 Flowers

  • Lavender peaks July 15–25 in Furano
  • Farm Tomita: arrive before 8 AM
  • Shikisai-no-oka: tractor rides available
  • Sunflowers face south by afternoon
  • Bring macro lens for close-ups

🎆 Fireworks

  • Confirm exact dates closer to travel
  • Arrive 1–2 hrs early for a good spot
  • Lake Toya: nightly Apr 28–Oct 31!
  • Bring ground sheet to sit on
  • Check local tourist boards for times

🌋 Lake Toya

  • Nightly fireworks — no schedule needed
  • Mt. Usu last erupted in 2000
  • Day-use onsen available at most hotels
  • Boat to Nakajima island from the pier
  • Bear spotted occasionally near Usu

🛍️ Sapporo Shopping

  • Tanukikoji: 7-block covered arcade
  • Sapporo Factory: Meiji-era brewery
  • Royce chocolate factory outlet
  • Shiroi Koibito flagship store
  • Lavender goods, sunflower honey, miso

Hokkaido in Full Bloom

Ten days of purple fields, golden sunflowers, festive nights, volcanic calderas, and fireworks over mountain lakes — ending with a hard-earned shopping spree in Sapporo.
