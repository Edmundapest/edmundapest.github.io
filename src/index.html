Before You Go

Trip Notes

🚗 Driving

  • International Driving Permit required
  • Book rental car 3+ months ahead
  • ETC card saves time at toll booths
  • Google Maps works perfectly offline
  • Hokkaido roads wide and well maintained

💜 Flowers

  • Lavender peaks July 15–25 in Furano
  • Farm Tomita: arrive before 8 AM
  • Shikisai-no-oka: tractor rides available
  • Sunflowers face south by afternoon
  • Bring macro lens for close-ups

🎆 Fireworks

  • Confirm exact dates closer to travel
  • Arrive 1–2 hrs early for a good spot
  • Bring a ground sheet to sit on
  • Shows last 45–90 minutes
  • Check local tourist boards for times

🏨 Staying

  • Book lodging 3+ months in advance
  • Furano ryokan fills fast in peak season
  • Convenience stores are lifelines
  • IC Suica card for Sapporo transit
  • Bear spray advisable on nature walks

🛍️ Sapporo Shopping

  • Tanukikoji: 7-block covered arcade
  • Sapporo Factory: former Meiji brewery
  • Royce chocolate factory outlet
  • Shiroi Koibito flagship store
  • Lavender goods, sunflower honey, miso

Hokkaido in Full Bloom

Ten days of purple fields, golden sunflowers, festive nights, and fireworks over mountain valleys — ending with a hard-earned shopping spree in Sapporo. This is Hokkaido summer at its most spectacular.
